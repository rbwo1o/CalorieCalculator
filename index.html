Calorie Calculator

This app helps you estimate the time required to reach your goal weight based on your current weight, daily calorie intake, and activity level. Simply input your details, and we'll calculate your projected progress. You can also view a graph that charts your weight loss or gain over time, providing a clear visual of your journey. Get started by entering your information and see how long it will take to achieve your goal.

This Calorie Calculator uses the Mifflin-St Jeor Equation, which is widely regarded as the most accurate method for calculating basal metabolic rate (BMR). The Mifflin-St Jeor Equation was introduced in 1990 and is preferred over older methods like the Harris-Benedict Equation.

Mifflin-St Jeor Equation:

Male:

BMR = 10W + 6.25H - 5A + 5

Female:

BMR = 10W + 6.25H - 5A - 161
